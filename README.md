## Floor Plan
The mock-restaurant floor plan consists of five different room sections. These room sections are:
* **Main section:** Loudest and busiest section in the restaurant, which offers seats for 4 - 6 customers. Meant for families and get-togethers.
* **Quiet section:** The most mellow and quiet section in the restaurant, which offers seats for 2 - 4 customers. Meant for dates and magical nights.
* **Terrace:** Outdoor section of the restaurant, which offers seats for up to 2 customers. Meant for warm summer days and clear stary nights.
* **Show section:** Section of the restaurant which includes a stage for performers and special events and offers seats for up to 2 customers. This section might not be available at all times.
* **Special section:** Two secluded rooms in the restaurant, where the larger rooms offer seats for up to 8 customers and the smaller room offers seats for up to 6 customers. Meant for private birthdays and other celebrations.
